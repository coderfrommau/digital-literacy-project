## Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Use strong passwords and do not share them with anyone
- Do not share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Always check UPI payment requests before approving
- Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages
- Verify calls or messages claiming to be from banks or apps
- Keep your apps and phone updated regularly
- Use two-factor authentication for accounts
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Log out from shared or public devices after use

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number: 1930
